    Giving Back

    About

    The Miriam Kayla Alumnae Initiative "Giving Back" will provide the Five Towns and Far Rockaway communities with monthly chessed events in addition to serving as a network for TAG alumnae to share upcoming chesed opportunities. “Giving Back” is a program created L’luy Nishmas Morah Miriam Adler, our beloved teacher at TAG H.S., to whom chesed played a vital role. This initiative will be a tool for alumnae to continue the TAG chessed legacy through “Giving Back” to the community, which has given so much to them.
